## The Split
- 4 minutes of running at the fastest pace you can maintain for 4 minutes.
- 4 minutes of rest.
- Repeated 4 times.
- Do this 2 times a week.

Other exercise routines can stay the same, as **cardio, weight lifting, etc**.
